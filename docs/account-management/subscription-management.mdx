## Cancel Subscription

To cancel your subscription, follow these steps:

1. **Navigate to My Plan**
   - Go to the **My Plan** section in your account settings.

2. **Click on Cancel Subscription**
   - Find and click on the **Cancel Subscription** button.

3. **Redirect to Stripe**
   - You will be redirected to the Stripe website.

4. **Follow the Stripe Workflow**
   - Complete the cancellation process following the steps provided by Stripe.
