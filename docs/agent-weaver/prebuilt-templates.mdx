> **Why start cold?** Weaver ships with dozens of ready-made flows. Two clicks and you’re 80 % done.

<InfoCallout>
**TL;DR:** Browse → Remix → Swap skills → Use as your own. 
</InfoCallout>

<Divider/>

## 1 · Browse the Componenet Library

1. Click **Templates** in the left sidebar.  
2. Filter by **category** (marketing, sales, dev).  
3. Click on a card to get a quick look at what the template does.

## 2 · Remix in One Click

*Click “Remix”* and Weaver drops the template onto your canvas:

- All skills are duplicated under a new agent ID (safe editing).  
- Variables (e.g., `{{api_key}}`) are highlighted for quick replacement.

> **Example:** Replace *OpenAI Chat* with *GPT-4o mini* to cut token spend by 80 %.

<Divider/>

### FAQ: Do my remixes update when the original template changes?

No. Remix creates a copy.
