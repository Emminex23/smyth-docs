# Competitor Researcher and Analysis Agent  

The **Competitor Researcher and Analysis Agent** helps you run **in-depth competitor research** with minimal effort.  
You provide two company names and their website URLs, and the agent will:  

- Search the web for competitor insights  
- Scrape both websites for detailed business information  
- Create a **structured competitor analysis report** including:  
  - Executive summary  
  - Company profiles  
  - Competitive strengths and weaknesses  
  - Strategic takeaways  

<InfoCallout title="Why use this agent?">  
This agent gives strategists, executives, and sales teams the **competitive intelligence they need** to make informed business decisions and improve positioning.  
</InfoCallout>  

<Spacer size="lg" />  

## Use Cases  

- **Strategic business planning**  
  Analyze competitors before entering new markets or launching products. Identify opportunities, threats, and differentiation strategies.  

- **Competitive intelligence & market analysis**  
  Track competitor activities, product launches, and positioning to stay ahead in your market.  

- **Sales enablement**  
  Equip your team with competitor insights so they can build stronger value propositions and handle objections with confidence.  

<TipCallout title="Pro tip">  
Use this agent to build **side-by-side competitor reports** that you can share directly with leadership or sales teams.  
</TipCallout>  

<Spacer size="lg" />  

## Testing the Agent  

### Step 1: Access the Agent  

<Image src="/docs/img/competitor_research/Homepage.jpg" alt="Competitor Researcher Agent template in SmythOS" />  

- Go to the **Templates** section in the sidebar  
- Open the **Marketing** tab  
- Find **Competitor Researcher and Analysis Agent** and click **Remix**  
<Image src="/docs/img/competitor_research/Remix.jpg" alt="Competitor Researcher Agent template Remix in SmythOS" />  
- The full workflow will open in Agent Studio  

<Spacer size="md" />  

### Step 2: Run the Agent  

You can test the agent in two ways:  

**Option 1: From the top toolbar**  

<Image src="/docs/img/competitor_research/Form Preview.jpg" alt="Testing Competitor Researcher Agent from toolbar" />  

1. Click **Test** (top-right)  
2. Switch to **Form Preview**  
3. Fill in the input fields  
4. Click **Run** to execute  

**Option 2: Form Preview from the canvas**  

1. Click the **Form Preview** button on the `generate-competitor-analysis` block  
2. Enter input details, for example:  
   - Company A Name: *Tech Innovations Inc*  
   - Company B Name: *Creative Solutions Ltd*  
   - Company A URL: *https://www.techinnovations.com*  
   - Company B URL: *https://www.creativesolutions.com*  
3. Click **Run** to generate the competitor analysis report

## Deploying the Agent  

### Step 1: Start Deployment  

<Image src="/docs/img/competitor_research/Deploy.jpg"  alt="Deploy button in SmythOS Agent Studio" />  

- Click **Deploy** (top-right corner of Agent Studio)  
- Pick your environment:  
  - **Agent Cloud** (default, fully managed)
  - **Enterprise** (self-managed, secure)
  - **Local Runtime** (for development and offline use)

<Spacer size="md" />  

### Step 2: Choose Your Deployment Type  

Pick how users will interact with your agent.

- **Custom GPT** – Add instructions, behaviors, or tools  
- **Chatbot** – Deploy as a chat interface  
- **LLM** – Connect to large language models with API keys  
- **API** – Call your agent programmatically  
- **MCP** – Use Model Context Protocol for structured workflows  
- **Alexa** – Launch as a voice assistant skill  

You can find detailed guides to them by reading the *[Deploy Your Agent As...](/docs/agent-deployments/deployments/)** page.

<InfoCallout title="Best option">  
For most users, deploy to **Cloud** and test via **Chatbot** to share competitor insights quickly across your team.  
</InfoCallout>

## Customization Tips  

<TipCallout title="Example customization">  
Want a **board-ready report**? Customize the analysis prompt to include an **executive summary, SWOT breakdown, pricing models, and recent press coverage** for each competitor.  
</TipCallout>  

- **Search volume** – Raise `sourcesLimit` from 3 to 8–12 for deeper analysis, or lower to 2 for faster comparisons  
- **AI models** – Use **GPT-5** for quality, **GPT-5-mini** for speed   
- **Analysis accuracy** – Lower temperature (0.2–0.4) for more precise reporting  
- **Report depth** – Add sections like *pricing strategies*, *market positioning*, or *technology stack comparisons*  
- **Time relevance** – Use `timeRange` (week, month, year) to focus on the latest competitor developments  
- **Scraping resilience** – Keep `antiScrapingProtection` and `javascriptRendering` enabled for protected business sites  
- **Additional sources** – Add LinkedIn pages, press releases, or investor reports for richer intelligence
